Umbrella

Created by Resh. Has an umbrella that is bullet-proof, has a sharp tip, can shoot explosive projectiles, and can fire as a grapnel. Also has a jetpack made of several blades that spread to look like wings. Radiation poisoning (to which he is immune) from the hydrogen cores in the umbrella cause his eyes to glow.

Tarantula

Created by Insanity. Can sprout and retract limbs from his back.
